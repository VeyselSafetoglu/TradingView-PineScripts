🏛️ Smart Money Concepts: Order Block & Liquidity Engine
This indicator is designed to follow in the footsteps of "Smart Money", avoiding the traps that retail traders fall into. It detects with high accuracy those critical areas (Order Blocks) where market makers enter their huge orders.

🔍 What is Order Block and Why is it Important?
Order Blocks are special candle structures where large financial institutions (banks, hedge funds) create imbalance in the market and the price changes direction sharply. When the price returns to these zones, it usually gives a "reaction" to complete the unfinished orders. This is our "Sniper" entry point!

🛠️ Highlights of the Indicator:
Automatic OB Detection: Instantly calculates Bearish and Bullish order blocks and processes them on the chart.

Break and Confirmation (MSB/BOS): Shows only valid blocks based on the points where the market structure changes (Market Structure Break).

Price Gaps (FVG): Provides compensation by working in integration with "Fair Value Gaps", which are evidence of corporate speed.

Visual Cleanliness: Focuses only on the freshest areas where the price can react, without polluting the screen.

📈 How to Use?
Bullish OB (Green Zone): Wait for the price to pull back to this zone. Pursue a buy position with wick rejection.

Bearish OB (Red Zone): Set up your sell strategy when the price hits this resistance block and shows weakness.

Risk Management: Protect your house by always placing your stop slightly above/below the block.

Remember: The market does not move randomly, it moves between liquidity and order blocks. This code offers you that map.
